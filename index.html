Why Athens?

Athens, historic city and capital of Greece. Many of Classical civilization's intellectual and artistic ideas originated there, and the city is generally considered to be the birthplace of Western civilization.

Athens, with its tall buildings and contemporary shops, is the first European city when approached from the Middle East. When approached from the west, from elsewhere in Europe, what strikes the visitor is the influence of the East—in the food, music, and clamorous street life—perhaps vestiges of a time when Athens was divorced from European society under the yoke of Ottoman rule. Nevertheless, it is wrong to say that Athens is a mixture of East and West: it is Greek and, more particularly, Athenian. The city, after all, nurtured Western civilization thousands of years ago. Athens remains on the world stage to this day.

My favorite cafes in Athens

Cafe City Zen

In the heart of the historic center, in a highly renovated and unique space curated by interior designer Nikos Angelidis, the "new" City Zen succeeds in relaxing you and taking you away from the intense rhythms of everyday life.

The all-day spot awaits you at the corner of Aiolos and Mitropoleos, offering coffee and brunch as well as special tasty food suggestions with an incomparable view of the Acropolis.

Chef Haris Bouris signs the menu inspired by Mediterranean cuisine with an emphasis on fresh ingredients and handmade preparations. Comfort food for all ages with vegetarian and vegan options. Traditional twisted recipes such as fried pumpkin and pastitsio that you must try.

CityZen is undoubtedly an excellent choice for your evening meetings as well, with a wide variety of spirits and a renewed cocktail list with imaginative mix suggestions from multi-award winning Bar Manager Yiannis Natsis.

Address:

Aiolou 11, Athens

What I like about it

This is one of the best brunch spots in Athens but if you go at any time of day there will be someting delicious on the menu!

360 Cafe

Found elevated above Monastiraki Square, 360 Cocktail Bar is a vibrant, trendy, and popular Athens rooftop bar and restaurant, offering a prime location with prime views.

Perched on top of the fantastic 360 Degrees Hotel, the spacious rooftop mixes an industrial-style interior with a lush rooftop garden. Really living up to its name, the terrace comes surrounded with amazing views over Acropolis, Parthenon and downtown Athens.

Up here you can enjoy a food menu taking you on a journey of tastes among Greek and Mediterranean cuisine, as well as a wide selection of signature cocktails taking inspiration from all over Greece, and a large array of spirits and wine.

Open from early day to late night, you can visit 360 Cocktail Bar for a sunny lunch, memorable sunset drinks, a relaxing dining experience, or vibrant nights under the Athens stars.

Address:

Ifestou 2, Athens

What I like about it

Vibrant bar with an industrial-style interior & a rooftop garden serving signature cocktails.

MS Cafe

MS Roof Garden is located in the heart of Athens, at Monastiraki, Athenas 1 and Ermou and you find the entrance from the Stoa next to the Train Station in Monastiraki. It is a new acquisition of the city and many are talking about the new hot spot which is ideal for all hours of the day.

You can drink your coffee, taste delicious dishes and even enjoy your cocktail with nice music and an even better view. At the MS Roof Garden, you can try grilled octopus with fava beans, arugula and salmon bruschetta, sauteed pork tenderloin, shrimp pasta and hearty salads, with a special feature.

The MS Special salad contains lettuce, arugula, goat cheese and is so delicious that you can choose it as a main course. The MS Roof Garden in Monastiraki also has incredible sweets. Don't resist the chocolate ball! Let the chocolate flow in your blood without fear!

Address:

Athinas 1 & Ermou, Athens

What I like about it

Vibrant eatery serving breakfast staples, burgers & meat plates, plus cocktails & Acropolis views.
